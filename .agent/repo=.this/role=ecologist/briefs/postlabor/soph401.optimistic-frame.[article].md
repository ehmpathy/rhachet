## .why

fear-based frames are counterproductive:
- they imply extractors are powerful
- they position distributors as underdogs
- they create adversarial dynamics

hope-based frames are generative:
- they assume distributed abundance is natural
- they position extractors as anachronistic
- they create collaborative momentum

the assumption: extraction is the default (iron law of oligarchy), but distributed preference is near-universal (99.999%). ratchets are entropic â€” they only click forward. the task is not to fight extractors but to accelerate distribution. extraction is archaic; the ratchets will make it obsolete.
