## R2: env=all must not expand to duplicates

**requirement**:
- SET: `--env all` stores under `$org.all.$key` only (no expansion)
- GET/UNLOCK: `--env prod` includes `$org.all.*` keys

**implementation**:
- `setKeyrackKey.ts`: removed env=all expansion logic, always uses single slug `${input.org}.${input.env}.${input.key}`
- `getAllKeyrackSlugsForEnv.ts`: changed filter from `spec.env === input.env` to `spec.env === input.env || spec.env === 'all'`

**test coverage**:
- `setKeyrackKey.test.ts`:
  - env=all stores single slug $org.all.$key ✓
  - (negative) env=all does NOT store under $org.prod.$key ✓
- `getAllKeyrackSlugsForEnv.test.ts`:
  - env=prod includes $org.all.$key slugs ✓
  - env=prod includes $org.prod.$key slugs ✓
  - (negative) env=prod does NOT include $org.test.$key slugs ✓
  - env=all returns all slugs (unchanged behavior) ✓

**verdict**: ✅ covered
