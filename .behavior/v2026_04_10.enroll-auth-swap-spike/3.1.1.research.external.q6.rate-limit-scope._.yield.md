# q6: rate limit scope — yield

## answer

**per-organization (API) / per-subscription (Claude subscriptions)** — NOT per-IP

## source

[Anthropic Rate Limits](https://platform.claude.com/docs/en/api/rate-limits)

> The API enforces service-configured limits at the **organization level**

> Rate limits are applied separately for each model

## implications

- rate limits are NOT per-IP — rotation across subscriptions will work
- each subscription account has its own independent rate limits
- rotation strategy is valid: when subscription A maxes out, switch to subscription B
- no IP-based throttle blocks the approach
